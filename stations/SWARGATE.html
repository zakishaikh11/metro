SWARGATE METRO STATION

Swargate station permits multi modal connectivity with inter-city and inter-state bus terminus. Commuters from both bus terminals can easily travel to concourse of station through network of dedicated subways. One of the entrances is proposed at Ganesh Kala Mandir, which would cater to flux of people generated from Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium. Swargate MMTH is planned with dedicated spaces for various vehicle modes like busses, four wheelers, two wheelers, rickshaws, cycle and pedestrian tracks. To minimize movement of pedestrians on the street, subway connectivity to MSRTC, PMPML and Metro has been planned.
