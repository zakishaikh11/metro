MANGALWAR PETH METRO STATION

This station is situated in Managalwar Peth, near RTO and AISSMS college campus. Mangalwar Peth was settled in 1662 when Shaista Khan came to Pune. Mangalwar Peth is a popular destination for the transportation business and hosts an old flea market, Juna Bazaar, on the banks of the river Mutha.
