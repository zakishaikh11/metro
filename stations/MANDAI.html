MANDAI METRO STATION

Mandai is an underground metro station. Mandai Station is planned near the existing Mahatma PhuleMandai, which is the biggest vegetable market in Pune city, a landmark. It is a densely populated area which heavily faces traffic congestions, accumulation of people and it is always busy.This metro station will provide connectivity to Shanipar, AppaBalwantChowk, DagadushethGanapati, Tulshibag, Kelkar Museum, RavivarPeth etc.
